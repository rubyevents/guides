# Ruby Community Guides

Practical, experience-backed guides for people who want to start and sustain local Ruby communities. Written from the lived experience of organizers who have done it — not from the outside looking in.

## Meetups

[Running Ruby Meetups](/meetups/)

How to start a local Ruby meetup from nothing, find a venue, get speakers, handle the logistics nobody talks about, and keep it going past the first year.
